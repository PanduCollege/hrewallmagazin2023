ABOUT

The wall magazine of the department of Human Rights Education “Opportunities Of Human Rights Education”

 was inaugurated on 23rd February, 2024 at 1.15pm. It was inaugurated by Dr Sanchay Jyoti Bora , Principal of Pandu College. Mrs joyeeta Das, assistant Professor of Human Rights Education Department, the faculty members and all the students of Human Rights Education were present in the programme.
The theme of the wall magazine for the year 2024 is "Opportunities Of Human Rights Education ". This magazine would be a platform for the students to evolve themselves in various prospects of personal and academic life.
